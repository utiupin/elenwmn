My Services

Color Analysis

Highly recommended analysis. I analyze inherent colors in your eyes and create a custom palette with detailed description of colors which work best in your outfits. You will also learn about your skin tone code and the best foundation products from Sephora specifically for your skin tone. Cost - $30

Wardrobe Consult

This consultation is done based on the current season trends or your personal preferences. It includes fashion personality style test, body shape analysis, color analysis and figure correction techniques. You will learn about mix and match varieties for your wardrobe, and receive a portfolio with daily outfit options. Cost - $40 per hour online or offline

Shopping Consult

We discuss your wardrobe and budget goals to create a shopping list based on your needs. You get a detailed consultation on current trends, color combinations and the best styling tips to flatter your figure. Color analysis is included. Shopping links are provided accordingly. Cost - $40 per hour online or offline

Styling Photoshoot

I create 2 - 4 fashion images based on the photoshoot theme and assist at the shoot scene styling all outfits and accessories. Hair and makeup consultation is included. Cost - $40 per hour
